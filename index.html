Privacy Policy

What these services are

Vade and Chalam are personal AI agents operated solely by the owner for personal use. They are not commercial products, not available to the public, and do not serve any users other than the individual who configured and operates them.

Data collected

These agents process only information the owner explicitly provides, including:

  • Inbound messages sent by the owner via WhatsApp or SMS
  • Voice notes sent by the owner (transcribed for processing)
  • Calendar, email, and contact data from the owner's own Google account
  • Financial data from the owner's own connected brokerage and bank accounts (read-only)

No data is collected from any third party. No data is collected passively or without the owner's direct action.

How data is used

All data is used solely to generate responses and perform tasks on behalf of the owner. This includes summarizing emails, reading calendars, checking portfolio balances, and responding to personal requests. Data is not used for advertising, analytics, model training, or any commercial purpose.

Data storage

Processed data and agent memory are stored on a private server controlled exclusively by the owner. No data is stored in third-party databases beyond what is required for API calls to services the owner has explicitly connected (Anthropic, Twilio, Google, Plaid). Each of those services is governed by its own privacy policy.

Third-party sharing

No data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party for any purpose. API calls to Anthropic, Google, Twilio, and Plaid are made solely to fulfill the owner's requests and are subject to those providers' own terms.

Retention

The owner controls all data retention directly. Memory files can be cleared, backed up, or deleted at any time by the owner.
